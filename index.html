About

Free and open-source

Z-Score offers a powerful, free, and open-source game backend solution for developers. Easily manage player data, leaderboards, achievements, currencies, and more with our scalable platform designed to streamline your game development process.

The main thing with Z-Score is an easy deployment as a game back-end and fast development for your games by using the Z-Score SDKs.
